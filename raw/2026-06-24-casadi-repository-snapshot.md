## Known project role

- CasADi is a symbolic framework commonly used for algorithmic differentiation and numerical optimization.
- It is often used as a modeling layer above solvers such as Ipopt and in MPC toolchains such as do-mpc and acados interfaces.

## Interpretation boundary

- CasADi is not itself a controller or planner.
- Its value is symbolic modeling, automatic differentiation and solver interface construction.
